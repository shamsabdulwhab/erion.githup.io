The first assignment (UCD)

1-Artistic intervention “How I see myself as UCD designer in the future”
2-Find three “good” websites and three “bad” websites and defend your stance about them.

The explaination

This poster was the first assignment.we had to explain three bad sites and three good sites from each person point of view . I also explained how I see myself as UCD designer

How I see myself as a UCD designer?

Although I am more interested in front-end and coding topics more, but when considering UCD topics. I see it as design-centric and requires patienceand users' understanding . I guess I'd have to do a lot of research to know the user, and the difficulty at this point is knowing what the users feels and needs, even if they don't give us clear answers in the user interview. I think this stage will make me nervous because I understand that there are things that will be thrown away, things that will be modified and things that will be replaced. I understand that working as a UCD is not the same as a linear process. So after each stage, I need to redefine and reasses my goals and assumptions. The other stages depend more on my creativity, how will I translate the requirements of the user's needs into a great design. And It is interesting because I'm the person who pays attention to the details
