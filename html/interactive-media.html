Prototype


First:After everygroup memeber showed its own prototype.We discussed which design we will use from every member. EX: we used header and footer of Ivana. Exibition-page we inspired from Alexandra. From my prototype we took the wireframe and gallery-page. Second:Create Low-Fidelity Wireframes. I've learned that low-fidelity is quick sketches that focus on layout and structure rather than visual design. They help to visualize the overall page structure and the placement of various elements. Its important that Instead of using actual content, I can use placeholder text such as "Lorem ipsum" or simple descriptive phrases to represent the general content structure. This helps convey the idea without getting caught up in the specifics of the text.



Finally After we finished the low fidelity prototype, we sat down together and started doing the interactive high fidelity prototype. While we were making the prototype we were challenged to incorporate two different ideas, sometimes we had two different opinions. Solutions: We ask people who aren't in our group, see which layout they like, and use that layout.
