StoryTelling activity

On June 5th, we discovered that storytelling is a skill that improves as we dedicate time and effort to practice. To enhance our storytelling abilities, we engaged in a group activity during our class. We participated in a delightful exercise that consisted of three key events: the beginning(A guy gets shot), middle(A flight starts in a bar), and end(The police is running to the bar). Interestingly, altering these events can completely transform the narrative we create.
